Purpose

The purpose of this site is to give weather conditions to user in a fast and easy manner. It will show you weather conditions for your current location and any location that you specify. It aims to make peoples days easier with an quick and reliable source for their weather needs. The weather information will look neat and easy to read. The site will be intuitive, pretty, and easy to navigate.
